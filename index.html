WHO WE ARE

At EVEN IF, we are here to help those who are committed to personal development realise and reach their full physical potential.

Our personalised workout programmes will ensure you make the most of the time you have available for exercise, and our nutritional guidance will help you develop positive habits that support your progress throughout your day-to-day life.

In short, our goal is to work with you, and work around your other commitments, to ensure that you attain the greatest possible return on your efforts, providing noticeable improvements to your body, your overall health and your self-confidence.

About Me

I first started training as skinny 13-year-old kid in 2010, with almost no existing muscle, no confidence and no knowledge at all regarding exercise or nutrition.

The only thing I did have was a newly found passion for fitness and a burning curiosity about how I would one day look and feel if I made a consistent effort to develop my fitness and my physique.

Fast forward to April 2025, and after 15 years of learning, growth and consistent hard work, I finally came to the realisation that, when it comes to fitness, there was always going to be another goal to set.

I realised that reaching one specific goal wasn’t the main reward for the hard work. The reward was all the other benefits that come with it: improved health, confidence, determination and discipline.

I realised that the gym was the one place where hard work and smart work will always produce rewards. Independent of anybody else’s approval or appreciation, the results will come.

After acknowledging this, it became clear that by sharing the knowledge and experience I had accumulated over 15 years, I could help other people understand what they are capable of and guide them towards their goals in a faster and smarter way.

I signed up to complete the relevant qualifications for personal training immediately. I also registered to complete two higher level qualifications to advance my knowledge of Nutrition and Strength & Conditioning.

I left my existing job in September, completed the qualifications and began building EVEN IF, which after months of hard work started trading in July 2026.

The Path to Progress

Your coaching journey is structured, supportive and designed to help you build momentum from the very first step.

Step 01

Enquire

Every journey is made up of smaller steps, and the first step is the smallest of them all.

Start the journey today by simply clicking ‘Enquire Now’ and completing the basic details.

Step 02

Register

Once we receive the completed enquiry form, we will send you an invite to register for the EVEN IF ‘Everfit’ app. Simply follow the instructions to download the app, create your account and log in.

Once you are logged in to the Everfit app, you will be prompted to complete a series of introductory forms which simply record necessary information about yourself, provide you with necessary information about us (Terms and Conditions etc.) and confirm that you are happy to proceed.

Step 03

Consultation & Programme

Once you have registered for your account on the Everfit app, we will contact you to arrange an initial consultation. This will allow us to get to know a little bit more about you, ask any relevant follow-up questions from the initial forms and answer any queries you have about the system or process so far.

Following the consultation, we will have all the information we need prepare your personalised programme.

Step 04

Ongoing Commitment

Over the course of the programme, we will work together to ensure you can stick to the structured programme as best as possible, managing obstacles or changes in circumstances as they come.

Step 05

Results

At the end of your programme, you will have made measurable progress towards your fitness goals and will have introduced positive new habits into your life.

You will have solid evidence that you can reach set milestones through small consistent efforts, along with deeper knowledge to better support your future goals, and in turn a significant boost to your self-confidence.
